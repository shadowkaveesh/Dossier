Spec 02 / Market Context

Macro Was Noisy.
Structure Was Readable.

The purpose was not to predict every release. It was to separate real directional pressure from temporary reaction and only act when price confirmed the view.

01 / MARKET CONTEXT

Policy ambiguity

Data surprises and policy uncertainty kept the dollar and euro repricing against one another. The focus stayed on whether price was respecting the underlying directional structure.

02 / ENTRY LOGIC

Proof before entry

The framework looked for a higher-low / higher-high sequence, a clean pullback into structure, and momentum confirmation. A level alone was never enough.

03 / RISK CONTROL

Defined first

The stop was placed before entry and size was calibrated to the quality of the setup. A promising idea still had to earn a controlled amount of exposure.

Spec 03 / Execution Register

Quarter Sequence

Early Q3
