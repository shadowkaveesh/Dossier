Spec 02 / Engineering

Built Around Systems

Aero, cooling, power delivery, and ride quality are treated as one linked programme rather than separate styling exercises.

01 / AIRFLOW

Stable at speed

Fastback shoulders, venturi tunnels, and splitter control work together to reduce lift while keeping highway behaviour predictable.

02 / THERMAL

Heat has a route

Separate coolant, oil, and charge-air paths are proposed to stop sustained load from becoming a single thermal bottleneck.

03 / ROAD LOGIC

Composure first

Adaptive damping and a low-mounted hybrid pack prioritise control on uneven Indian roads without losing the GT brief.

Spec 03 / System Register

Design Checklist
