01 / SIDE PROFILE

Fastback shoulders, venturi underbody shaping, and a sculpted roofline tuned to reduce turbulence at 110–140 km/h highway loads.

02 / UNDERBODY FLOW

Underbody tunnels, diffuser wake management, and front splitter pressure balancing are designed to keep lift in check and cornering stability high.

03 / POWER / THERMAL

High-output inline-four architecture, active thermal routing, and a torque-vectoring control strategy tuned for both low-speed city bite and highway stability.

04 / CFD PRESSURE MAP

Pressure gradients are balanced across the nose, mirror pods, and rear section to lower wake separation and stabilise the chassis under crosswind loading.

05 / THERMAL ROUTING

Heat flow mapping keeps the core loop inside the 95–100°C window, with separate charge-air and oil cooling tiers to prevent thermal coupling under prolonged track work.

Chassis Logic

Double-wishbone front, multilink rear, active dampers, and a low-mounted battery backbone to keep roll center control clean and predictable.

Interior Philosophy

Minimalist cabin, driver-first controls, haptics, and premium materials with ergonomic architecture calibrated for Indian road use and long-distance comfort.

Production Narrative

Every major panel, bracket, and trim element is cost-scoped around a 45-lakh INR ceiling so the concept remains technically rational rather than purely aspirational.
